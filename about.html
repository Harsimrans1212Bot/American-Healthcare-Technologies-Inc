Home/About

Built for the tech room, not the sales deck.

AHT is a family-owned imaging equipment company. We buy, service, install, and occasionally un-install diagnostic imaging systems for hospitals and imaging centers.

Founded 1992Headquarters [Placeholder city]Ownership Family-owned

Placeholder story — AHT started in 1992 out of a single warehouse bay with one refurbished MRI and a rented forklift. Thirty-three years later we've deliveredered 500+ imaging systems to facilities across North America.

We exist because every imaging facility — big or small — needs someone on the other end of the phone who actually knows their scanner. Not a call center. Not a sales rep. An engineer.

Every system we sell is serialized, documented, and warrantied. Every service contract is signed by the people who will actually show up. And every part we ship has been physically tested on a like-model system before it leaves our dock.

We're not trying to be the biggest imaging equipment company. We're trying to be the one your biomed team keeps on speed-dial.

01 / Values

What we commit to.

Not slogans — standards. If we fail any of these, the invoice is refundable.

01

Document everything

Tube counts, boil-off logs, PM history, phantom results. On paper, on every unit, before money moves.

02

Engineer on the phone

The person who picks up at 2am can actually diagnose your system. Not transfer, not ticket. Diagnose.

03

Honest refurbishment

If a coil needs replacing to meet spec, we replace the coil. We do not "clean and re-crate."

04

Stay on schedule

First-patient-scan dates get written into the PO. Miss the date, we eat the downtime cost.

05

One PM per project

One phone number, one name, one email. From site survey to first scan and past go-live.

06

Leave the room better

De-install jobs end with a room that's cleaner, safer and more re-usable than we found it.

02 / Team

Who you'll be talking to.

Small team. You'll speak to the person who actually owns the answer — not a call center.
