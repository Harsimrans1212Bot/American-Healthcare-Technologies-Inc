Privacy policy.

Effective July 1, 2026 · American Healthcare Technologies, Inc. ("AHT", "we", "us")

What we collect

When you contact us through the form on this site, we collect the information you provide: your name, company or facility, email address, phone number, and the contents of your message. Our hosting provider may also record standard technical logs (such as IP address and browser type) needed to serve the site securely.

How we use it

We use your information for one purpose: to respond to your inquiry and, if you become a customer, to provide equipment, service, parts, and installation. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing.

Form processing

Form submissions are delivered to our email inbox by a third-party form-delivery service. The submission is transmitted for delivery purposes only.

Cookies and analytics

This site does not set marketing cookies and does not run third-party advertising or analytics trackers.

Retention and your rights

We keep inquiry records only as long as needed for business and legal purposes. You may request a copy of the information we hold about you, or ask us to correct or delete it, by emailing info@ahtiny.com.
