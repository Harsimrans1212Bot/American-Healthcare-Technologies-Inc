01 / Services

What we handle.

Full-service or any piece of it. Most clients take the whole package; a few prefer to run the GC themselves.

Installation

New-site, replacement, or relocation. We handle the magnet, the console, and everything in between.

  • Rigging and crane coordination
  • RF shielded room construction
  • Cryogen delivery, fill and ramp
  • Power, HVAC, chilled-water tie-ins
  • DICOM / PACS / EMR integration
  • Applications training (2–5 days)

De-installation

Responsible decommissioning, removal and disposal — with options to trade-in against your next system.

  • Cryogen ramp-down and recovery
  • Magnet crate, rig and transport
  • Room restoration to specification
  • Hazmat and lead-shielding disposal
  • Consigned resale option
  • Trade-in credit against next buy
